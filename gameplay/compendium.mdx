The skill compendium consists of four categories. Three of these categories show the progress of your skills and unlock specific locations & NPC vendors. The fourth category shows how many blueprints you have studied and unlocks different crafting & cooking recipes.

## Fish Compendium

The "Fish" compendium shows which of the fish you have already caught once. Filling it up to 50% will give you access to the gated area on the Koilot island. Filling it up to 100% will let you trade with Sgt. Lake (NPC Vendor).

## Feathers & Trophies Compendium

The "Feathers & Trophies" compendium shows which of the feathers and/or trophies you have already got from hunting. Filling it up to 50% will give you access to the gated area on the Padva island. Filling it up to 100% will let you trade with Filemon (NPC Vendor).

  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/yDLu2xx.png"
/>

## Plants Compendium

The "Plants" compendium shows which of the feathers and/or trophies you have already got from hunting. Filling it up to 50% will give you access to the gated area on the Padva island. Filling it up to 100% will let you trade with Filemon (NPC Vendor).

  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}

## Blueprints Compendium

The "Blueprints" compendium shows which blueprints you have already studied. Every studied blueprint will unlock a new crafting or cooking recipe. Filling it up to 50 or 100% will currently not unlock in anything.
