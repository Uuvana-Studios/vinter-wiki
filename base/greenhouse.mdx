The greenhouse can be bought for ~23.000 Mk from Angel (NPC Vendor) on the Heisala island. It is used to farm crops for you automatically, you only have to put in seeds once and it will never need new ones. It produces a maximum of 33 crops until its storage is full.

**Frequently asked question:**

How can I take out the seeds from the greenhouse? - Turn off auto plant. Once the plants are done growing after disabeling it, you can take out the seeds.
