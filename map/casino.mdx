The casino is a mini "bunker" located on top of the lighthouse where you only need to kill one guard but get rewarded with a lot of money as either the money stack item which is worth around 500 Mk or a goldbar which is wort around 2.500 Mk.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/1BL8Z0r.png"
/>

## Access

The casino does require an Access Card: Casino to enter.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/VMQ8VDh.png"
/>

## Casino loot

The casino loot consists of stacks of money and goldbars. The NPC guarding the casino has a chance to drop an access card as well.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/n8z1p9k.png"
/>

## Selling the stacks of cash

You can find a vending machine in the lobby of the casino called Vendo-O where you can sell your looted stacks of cash for ~500 Mk each.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/BXmEEhl.png"
/>

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://i.imgur.com/K1m87LI.png"
/>

## Non restricted loot

There are four containers in the basement of the lighthouse which contain common crate loot but do not require an access card to get to.

  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
