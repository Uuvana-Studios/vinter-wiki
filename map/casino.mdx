<Warning>
  The casino requires an Access Card: Casino to enter. Plan your visit accordingly!
</Warning>

The casino is a mini "bunker" located on top of the lighthouse, offering high-value loot with minimal resistance.

## Access

<Tip>
  An Access Card: Casino is required to enter the main casino area.
</Tip>
