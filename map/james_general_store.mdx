<Warning>
  While James General Store accepts almost all items, selling all items here may result in lower profits than compared to other dedicated shops.
</Warning>

James General Store is the cornerstone of commerce in Longvinter. Its unique position in the game's economy makes it an essential shop for all players.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Universal Buyer" icon="cart-shopping">
    Accepts nearly all items in the game, with few exceptions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Player-Driven Inventory" icon="people-arrows">
    Sells items that other players have sold to the store in the last 12 hours.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Trading Tips

<Tip>
  For maximum profit, consider selling specialized items (like fish or crafted goods) to their respective dedicated shops instead of James General Store.
</Tip>

1. Use James General Store to quickly sell miscellaneous items.
2. Check the store regularly for rare items sold by other players.
3. Remember that item availability changes based on player activity.

## Buying & Selling
You can switch between buying and selling items by clicking the "Buy" and "Sell" buttons at the top of the shop's menu.
